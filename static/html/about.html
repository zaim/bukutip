About BukuTip

Welcome! BukuTip is a free web app that searches for and compare book prices from various bookstores and websites in Malaysia.

BukuTip currently searches for book prices from MPH, Kinokuniya and Times Bookstores. It also searches BookXcess and Mudah.my which have cheaper prices (most probably) but with lesser book titles to search from.

BETA MODE: BukuTip is still new and is under heavy beta development, please bear with us if you encounter any errors or weird behaviours. Any server errors are automatically logged and notified to the developer, but if you'd like, you may contact him directly to report anything else.

How to search for a book

The best way to look for a book in BukuTip is to use it's ISBN (also known as EAN-13) number, you can find this number at the back of the book near the barcode. It is a 13-digit number which usually starts with 978, the "bookland" code. See the sample pic on the right.

You can also search using the book's title, author or keywords. When you do this however, the result might include editions published outside of Malaysia and therefore the book might not be available in local bookstores.

How's it done?

BukuTip simply does a direct search on each of the above bookstore's website for a book based on its ISBN, then parses (or scrapes) for the price value.

NOTE: For bookstore webmasters, please contact me if you'd like to know more about how we scrape you or if you'd like your bookstore to be featured also.
